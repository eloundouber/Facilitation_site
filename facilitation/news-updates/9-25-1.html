Friday Five: 417's Best Events 10/30 - 11/5

Share

~SPOOOOKY~

Halloween has arrived! We have provided a mixture of Halloween, fall, and holiday activities to get you through this weekend and week! Have a safe and fun time!

What's Happening This Week?

Ozark's Trunk or Treat

Where: Ozark's Downtown Square

When: October 31st from 5-8 PM


A fun, free Halloween event on Oct 31st, 2020! Trick or treat decorated trunks and downtown businesses.
• Enjoy free hot dogs, chips, and drinks brought to you by the Ozark Police Dept, Ozark Fire District, Christian County Sheriff's Dept, Christian County Ambulance District, Hometown Sports Print House, and CCCC Sertoma.
• Groups needed to provide trick or treating fun! • Businesses •Clubs • Families •Churches - Decorate your trunk or booth and help us make Halloween a great night for area kids!
Prizes will be awarded for 1st and 2nd place best-decorated trunk in each division: •Business

Holiday Extravaganza

Where: Ozark's Downtown

When: Thursday, Nov 5th - Saturday, Nov 7th from 10 AM - 5 PM

"Take a walk Downtown Ozark to shop our Christmas Open House! A variety of stores & restaurants will be open!
Lots of free gifts, discounts, giveaways and MORE!
Thursday the 5th there will be a Wine & Art walk from 5-8 P.M.

Participating Stores...

-Nest Boutique
-Market House Cafe
-Enchanted Parlor
-Hazel's Flowers
-Big Al's
-Torn Boutique
-Two Saved Sisters
-Huckabella
-Texas Soul
-Hometown Sports
-Turquoise Trunk
-Stacey's Sweets
-The Finley


Everybody can participate in the Grand Prize Entry to have a chance to win Downtown Bucks to spend at any participating stores!."

Barn Dance Under the Stars

Where: Sycamore Creek Family Ranch

When: Friday, October 30th from 7 PM - 10 PM

Fun barn dance outside under the stars! Put on your dancing boots and go check it out!

MOJO LITE at HOld Fast Brewing

Where: Hold Fast Brewing

When: Tuesday, Nov 3rd from 7-9 PM

"MOJO is coming to HOLD FAST!

Every Tuesday night through November from 7pm - 9pm, visit our open air taproom and enjoy the music of the Missouri Jazz Orchestra.

Tuesdays are also Taco Tuesdays with Tinga Tacos! So come on out, grab a beer, grab a taco, and tap your feet to the hottest jazz band in town."

Field of Screams Haunted Trail & Forest

Where: Summers at the River

When: Friday, October 30th from 7-11:59 PM

"Southwest Missouri's Totally Redesigned Haunted Trail & Forest. We've acquired another haunted attraction and incorporated it into our over 1/2 mile trail which bends through the forest and along the river. Nothing will be the same as last year. In addition to the redesign of our attraction, we'll also be taking many safety precautions. The safety of our staff and customers is our number 1 priority."
