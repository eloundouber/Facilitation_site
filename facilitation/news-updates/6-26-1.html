Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/3 - 7/9

Share

Who Doesn't Love A Good Firework Show?

No matter the circumstances it's time to celebrate the birth of America! Head outdoors and enjoy the numerous firework shows happening this weekend!

What's Happening This Week?

Liberty Light Up Concert & Fireworks

Where: Branson Landing, 100 Branson Landing Blvd, Branson, MO 65616

When: Friday, July 3rd from 4 - 10 PM

Liberty Light Up is a free event!
Join us for Branson Landing's 13th Annual Liberty Light Up – and celebrate Independence Day – in a BIG way a day early! The Liberty Light Up Concert (6 pm) will feature Mile Zero and The Dirty Saints, followed by a fantastic choreographed Fireworks Display that will fill the skies over Lake Taneycomo and highlight the spectacular celebration of America's Independence!
DJ Broc will be our emcee and DJ for the evening!

BEER GARDEN OPENS AT 5:00 pm bring your lawn chairs & blankets!

We encourage everyone to wear a mask. If you don't have a mask, we'll be handing them out before and during the event. We also encourage everyone to social distance and respect other people's personal space. Social distancing is the responsibility of each individual. We've also added 20+ hand sanitizing stations throughout the Landing.

Rockin' Rockaway Beach 4th of July Celebration 2020

Where: 2762 State Hwy 176, Rockaway Beach, MO

When: Saturday, July 4th, from12 PM - 11 PM

Celebrate the birth of the nation with live music from the Black Note and fireworks starting at 9:30 p.m. at Rockaway Beach. Entrance to the event is free and starts at noon. Be sure to bring some cash in case you want to check out the craft and food vendors, or let the kids have a chance at the carnival games.

Fireburst 2020 Table Rock Lake Fireworks - Kimberling City

Where: Visit Table Rock Lake, 14226 State Highway 13, Kimberling City, MO

When: Friday, July 3rd from 9 - 9:30 PM

Fireburst is an over-30-year tradition at Table Rock Lake, sponsored by Table Rock Lake Chamber of Commerce. Scheduled for July 3, 2020 near the Kimberling City bridge, this fireworks show is solely sponsored by community and business donations. The show can be viewed by land or by boat and is one of the area's most popular events. Please donate today!

Click here to donate!

Moon Light SUP Yoga + Meditation

Where: Ozarks SUP Yoga, Branson, MO

When: Sunday, July 5th @ 8 PM

Join us for a gentle yoga flow & guided meditation under the Buck Moon & a penumbral lunar eclipse.
Standup Paddle Board (SUP) Yoga is a peaceful yet fun way to take your practice outdoors and onto a floating mat. Our classes are designed to help you feel grounded and stable in this calming, yet empowering, flow.
We begin all our classes on land with a few minutes of instructions on SUP 101 and safety. Then paddle out to a cove to anchor our Floating Studio.

Total length in time is 75 minutes
Limited boards available
$30 board/class or $15 BYOB (bring your own board)
*Must pre-register for class
Call or Text with any questions (417) 230-7551

Cancellation Policy
If class is cancelled because of the weather conditions, it will be scheduled for a later date and an option to receive your full refund back.
*We require 24 hr cancellation notice prior to your scheduled SUP Yoga class for a refund. If you cancel the same day as your scheduled SUP Yoga class there will be no refund issued.

Sizzlin' Summer w/ Robert Madu

Where: James River Church, 6100 N 19th St, Ozark, MO 65721

When: Sunday, July 5th @ 9:00 AM, 10:45 AM, or 12:30 PM

Join us July 5th as we welcome Robert Madu to James River Church for our Sizzlin’ Summer series! You won’t want to miss this incredible Sunday as we come together for a time of powerful worship and an exciting message!

Be sure to share this event to invite your friends and family to join us for this incredible service!
