Friday Five: 417's Best Events 6/26 - 7/2

Share

Run, Sun, and some FUN!

The weather is clear and it's time for another amazing weekend! Get outside, stretch, breathe in the fresh air, and enjoy the time off! Check out this week's blog post to see what events you can join right in your back yard!

Don't miss out on new opportunities to get active!

What's Happening This Week?

Finley River Night Light 5k

Where: Ozark Community Center, 1530 W Jackson St, Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, June 27th from 8:30 - 11:30 PM

The Night Light 5k is a mix between a color run and a neon run, it can't get any better than that!

The First Annual Finley River Night Light 5k is going to be an absolute BLAST! The 5k will be marked along the way with Neon LED Balloons and neon glow tape so there is no way to lose your way! There will be black lights to LIGHT YOU UP, volunteers throughout the race with glow powder to cover you head to toe, and music bumping to make this run party even more fun! Before and after the music will shake you up all through the night. The end of the race will be just as much fun as the run, complete with glowing refreshments, music, lights, and a cool neon picture spot. WE CAN'T WAIT.

For more details and to sign up click here!

Ignite Float Trip

Where: Nixa First Assembly, 113 W Mt Vernon St, Nixa, MO

When: Saturday, June 27th from 12 - 3 PM

Bring a kayak or river float on June 27th! We will be meeting at the church at noon, then going to Shelvin Rock on the James River. Together, we will be floating for about 1 1/2 hours to 2 hours. Bring a friend, we can't wait to see you!

On Course For A Cause

Where: Highland Springs Country Club, 5400 S Highland Springs Blvd, Springfield MO

When: Monday, June 29th from 10:30 AM - 3:30 PM

Join us on the golf course at Highland Springs Country Club. Tee-off is at 10:30 am. The price per team is $600. Light brunch is provided to players.
All proceeds will go to the School-Age Service financial assistance. At the Y, we believe communities are stronger when everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential. Over the past year, the Ozarks Regional YMCA's School Age Service department served almost 3,000 children through childcare via Summer Day Camp, Before & After School, and Play Days.

For more information click here!

Horseback Riding + Winery

Where: 37 North Expeditions

When: Sunday, June 28th from 9:15 AM - 3:15 PM

Join us for a day of horseback riding and wine tasting with a great view of the Ozarks. We will saddle up after a short safety briefing that will let you get acquainted with your horse. We will go on an hour-long ride through the fields, woods, and creeks with an experienced guide. This experience is perfect for new horseback riders and experienced riders looking for a relaxing day. The views along the ride will not disappoint you.

After, we will drive to Bear Creek Winery, a great family-friendly winery, surrounded by fantastic views of the Ozarks. We will enjoy a complimentary tasting of amazing red and white wines. After the tasting, they also have rustic pizza, smoked meats, cheese, and local chocolate for you to choose whatever your heart desires while we relax and socialize at their amazing space. If wine is not your drink of choice, they brew their own beer and it is fantastic!

*All riders should be under 250 lbs.

Click here to sign up!

Karaoke Night at Back Home

Where: Back Home Brewing,1502 S 3rd St, Ozark, MO

When: Friday, June 26th from 8-11 PM

Karaoke is back with DJ Royce!!! Come sing your heart out! It starts at 8 pm!
