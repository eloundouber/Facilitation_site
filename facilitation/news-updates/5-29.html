Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/29 - 6/4

Share

Bright Shining Sun ☀️

The sun will be out and shining bright this weekend! Go outside and enjoy the weather! We have many options this week from paddle boarding, live music outdoors, eating delicious pie, or rocking the dock! Grab your friends and family and head outside!

What's Happening This Week?

Rock the Dock w/ Eastern Heights

Where: The Paddlewheel, 9 South Boardwalk, Branson, MO

When: Friday, May 29th from 7 PM - 11 PM

"NO COVER!
Come Rock The Dock with Modern Country Group Eastern Heights! A little country, a little rock, ALOT OF FUN! ."

Thursday Sunset Yoga with Bailey Humble

Where: Finley River Park, 891 N Riverside Rd., Ozark, MO

When: Thursday, June 4 @ 6 PM

Cost: $35.00 for one-hour session - Post Yoga paddles will be $10 Extra

Basic SUP Yoga with Bailey Humble. Bailey Is a trick rider, formerly with Dixie Stampede, she now travels the country showing off her skills at various events. We have been lucky enough to partner with Bailey to bring you weekly SUP Yoga.

Dani's Flour Pot Bakery (Grand Opening)

Where: 2852 W Republic Rd, Springfield, MO 65807

When: Tuesday, June 2nd from 9 AM - 3 PM

"The Pie Wagon is here!! Our first day open will be Tuesday, June 2nd, from 9-3 (or until supplies last). We are VERY excited to bring pie to you and even more excited to see all of your faces!! We will have Sweet 5”, 9”, and pie by the slice available. We will also have Savory 3” pies hot ready for you to eat and will still offer 5” frozen take and bake savory pies! We will also be offering limited drinks and a special meal deal of $12 that includes a 3” hot savory pie, small side salad, and a slice of sweet pie of your choosing! Don’t miss out! This is going to be a fun and relaxing place where you can come and eat pie outside or take it to go. There will be limited seating outside spaced 6 feet apart and sanitized after each use. Sit in a chair or bring a picnic table and eat some pie. The first official pie establishment has arrived and is waiting to fill your bellies!

You can as always, still pre-order pies!"

Denzil and Jonathan Band

Where: Vineyard Market, 1759 West St Hwy J, Ozark, MO 6572

When: Saturday, May 30th @ 7PM - 10 PM

"The Band will be playing outside as long as weather permits. If the weather doesn't cooperate, we will be moving inside."

Live with Randy & Laura

Where: The Clever Barn, 3647 Jasmine Rd, MO 65631

When: Saturday, May 30th @ 8PM - 9PM

This IS our " Happy Place" and we do try to "Scatter Kindness" just like our signs say❤️ Hope y'all can join us for our li'l party with some live music, laughs, and lotsa love 😊 We never said that we would be perfect but we sure do have FUN. If you like our "Happy Place" will you please ask your friends to tune in? Our goal is to grow this into having small business owners and other local musicians join us🎶🎉🍷🙏🏻😁 Just make sure to click on "Going" or "Interested" so you will get that notification when we go "Live"
