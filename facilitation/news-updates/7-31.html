Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/31- 8/6

Share

This one's for you, music lovers.

It is a big weekend for the Ozark Empire Fair Grounds. For all the music lovers out there the fun is back! You don't want to miss out on the amazing things this weekend has to offer. You are welcome in advance.

What's Happening This Week?

O'Reilly Auto Parts Outlaw Nationals Monster Truck Show

Where: Ozark Empire Fair, 3001 N Grant Ave, Springfield, MO

When: Saturday, August 1st from 7 PM - 10 PM

"Join us and 5 of the Nations top monster trucks in an all-out war!! Plus Stock and Pro-Tuff Truck classes! The O'Reilly Auto Parts Outlaw Nationals Monster Truck Show returns August 1st to Summer's Biggest Party! "

Mask Compliant

Click here to get your tickets!

Live at Millsap Farm Pizza Night - Tyler Acosta Music

Where: Millsap Farm, 6593 N Emu Ln, Springfield, MO

When: Saturday, August 1st from 6 PM - 8:30 PM

"Join us at Millsap Farm for an unlimited buffet of unique pizzas with farm-grown organic ingredients, featuring an all-new live set from Tyler Acosta Music! Tyler will be playing and singing songs from a wide variety of genres and time periods, and will be taking song requests via text during the show!

PRICING:
Adults $15.00
Children 4-12 yr old $6 per child
Toddler 0-3 yr old Free
Gluten-free options available, see website
Seating and capacity have been adjusted to allow for social distancing, servers will be wearing masks for your protection

Spots are limited and going fast, so reserve now using the link above! Payments will be taken at the door. A wide variety of fresh locally-baked cookies, as well as Tyler's CDs and other merchandise, will also be available for purchase."
