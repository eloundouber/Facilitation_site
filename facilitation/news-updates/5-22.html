Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/22-5/28

Share

To the Brave

We may not be sure what the weather will look like this weekend ( fingers crossed we have some changes) but we do know that this is a special weekend - to spend with friends and family remembering those who gave up their lives to protect ours. Stay safe, happy, & healthy!

What's Happening This Week?

Cornhole Yard Game - Limited Seating Workshop

Where: AR Workshop Springfield

When: Friday, May 22nd @ 6-8:30 PM

The perfect indoor activity to hide from this weekend's weather and you get to go home with the perfect at-home game!

"Fun and Easy Corn Hole yARd Game project for a special date-night to kick off summer! Enjoy making this two-person project with your favorite someone from AR Workshop Springfield!"

Pie & Flowers Holiday Pick-Up

Where: Vito's Kitchen

When: Friday, May 22 & Saturday, May 23 from 9 AM - 5 PM

"Pie & Flowers for your Memorial Day weekend celebrations!

Place your order for pie and flowers @ prairiepie.com/order
Pick-ups available at Vito's Kitchen on Friday, May 22nd and Saturday, May 23rd from 9 AM to 5 PM
Floral bouquets by Sarah Stracke
Pie by Prairie Pie

*pre-orders only for this event!"

Memorial May Carry the Load Hike

Where: Ozark Park Trail, 802 W Jackson St, Ozark, MO 65721

When: Saturday, May 23rd from 10:30 AM - 1:30 PM

To honor those who have sacrificed for us – our Military, Law Enforcement, Fire/ Rescue, and Emergency Medical personnel – Sheep Dog Impact Assistance Southern Missouri and Carry the Load invite you to join in our organized 2.5 mile hike along the Ozark Park Trail in Ozark, MO, on May 23, 2020. Safety briefing will start at 10:30 and the hike will commence at 11:00. Following the hike, join us for a cookout at the Finley River Park!

Please join the Sheep Dog Impact Assistance Carry the Load team here: https://www.carrytheload.org/site/TR/2019/General?team_id=6531&pg=team&fr_id=1740

In order to show your support and honor those Sheep Dogs who have served or are serving, we are offering t-shirts, name plates, and back packs to carry the names of those we wish to honor. We have three options to choose from:
Bundle 1: T-shirt $15
Bundle 2: T-shirt, SDIA shield patch, and Carry the Load Patch $23
Bundle 3: T-shirt, Propper backpack with Velcro, SDIA shield patch, and Carry the Load patch $45
Name plates $5 each. For the Sheep Dog you would like to honor.
Please include proper spelling of last name, branch of service or first responder field, and if they served in WWI or WWII.
SDIA water bottle $5
Please have orders is by May 1st to guarantee delivery!!

If you have any questions, please contact Chris Kozisek, 417-229-3180, Ckozisek@sheepdogia.org.

Chateau on the Lake Memorial Day Celebrations

Where: Chateau on the Lake, 415 N State Highway 265, Branson, MO

When: May 22nd-May 25th 9 AM - 9 PM

"On the lookout for a way to spend your long weekend this Memorial Day? Come out for a full weekend of summer fun at Chateau on the Lake in Branson. You can stay at the hotel for complimentary access to the events or just come up for the day. Bring the kids in the morning for some family activities or spend an evening out at the Chateau Grille. Enjoy live music and the classic fireworks show to finish out the weekend of celebration."

The Finley Brunch

Where: The Finley - Ozark, MO

When: Saturday's & Sunday's from 10 AM - 2 PM

There's a new brunch spot in town! Check out The Finley in Ozark for brunch on the weekends!
